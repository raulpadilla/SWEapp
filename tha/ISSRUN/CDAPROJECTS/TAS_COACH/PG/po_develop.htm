Develop Strategy Variations:

Develop - Invest:
This variation involves developing a presence for future possible opportunities by exploring the customer’s needs and establishing credibility within the account. Elements may include:

  • No compelling event exists
  • Establish a presence, listen and wait
  • Investment Marketing

Develop - Delay:
This variation involves slowing the customer’s buying decision by selling attractive future alternatives. Elements may include:

  • Not in a position to compete
  • Attractive future alternative
  • Requires inside support
