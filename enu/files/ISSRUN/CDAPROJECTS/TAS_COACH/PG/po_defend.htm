Defend Strategy Variations:

Defend-Insulate::
You have to continually build a fortress of business value for the customer so that they deny the competition any opportunity. Elements may include:

  • Improve your relationships
  • Support your allies
  • Extend your presence

Defend - Isolate:
If the competition has a foot in the door, you defend your ground by isolating the competition and containing their efforts to one department or one application. Elements may include:

  • Contain the competition
  • Create tangents/diversions
  • Dilute the competitor’s efforts
