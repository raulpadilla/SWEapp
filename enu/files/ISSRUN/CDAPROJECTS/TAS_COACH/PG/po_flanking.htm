Flanking Strategy Variations:

Flanking –Alter the Rules:
This variation involves changing the buying criteria from one set to another. In essence you are moving the goal posts to favor your solution. Elements may include:

  • Change criteria from A to B
  • Change or reprioritize the criteria
  • Move the goal post

Flanking – Acknowledge and Expand:
Instead of completely changing the rules, this variation involves expanding the buying criteria. Elements may include:

  • Change criteria from A to A+1
  • Expand the scope of the decision
  • Add new criteria
