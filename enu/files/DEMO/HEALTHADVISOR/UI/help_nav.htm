Contents list

When you first run a Rainmaker Sales application, a list of topics about the products available for selection and/or configuration is displayed. (This collection of topics is referred to as the Contents list.) Clicking a topic will expand or collapse a list of topics, or if the topic is linked to a product page, the page is displayed in the Inputs area.

The Contents list may be visible when the Rainmaker Sales application is first started, and it may appear in the Results area.


Results area

As you make selections in the Inputs area, you will get immediate feedback on whether a selection is part of a valid product configuration. (A valid configuration refers to the set of features that represent an available product.) This feedback appears in the Results area. If you make a selection that is not compatible with previous selections, the Results area will display suggestions that will help you make an appropriate choice. While you are making your requirement selections, this area may also display upgrades, promotions, and other beneficial product details.

Once you have selected all of the necessary requirements, the Results area displays information about the product that matches the requirement set.




Inputs area

The Inputs area is located to the right of the Results area and, when you first start the Rainmaker Sales application, it displays an introduction page such as Welcome. When you click on a topic in the Contents list that is linked to an Inputs page, the page will be displayed in this area.

An Inputs page may contain several types of controls that allow you to specify the type of product you need. Each time you make a selection, the Results area provides immediate feedback (see Results area for details). For information about the controls available in the Rainmaker Sales application, click on a topic under Selection Controls in this help.


Order button

Click the order button to add the currently configured product to an order. To order more than one of the same product, enter the desired number in the quantity entry box next to the product description. To remove an item from the order, type '0' (zero) in the quantity entry box.

The order information remains active during the entire Rainmaker Sales session, allowing you to add multiple products to the order. Each time you configure a product and click the order button, the product is added the order. Note, however, that once you exit and reload the Rainmaker Sales application, a new order is created.

The order may be printed and faxed or mailed to the company, or it may be submitted electronically.


Contents list button

If the Contents list is not visible, click on the button (commonly named Contents List or Products List) to display the list of products and topics provided in this Rainmaker Sales application.


Help button

Click on the Help button to display this information.


About button

Click on the About button to display information about the company whose Rainmaker Sales application you are using.
