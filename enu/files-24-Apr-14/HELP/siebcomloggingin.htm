Logging In

On Web sites that require you to log in, a login form appears. Once you have successfully logged in, the login form disappears.

To log in
  1. Navigate to the Web site.
  2. In the login form, enter your User ID and Password.
  3. Click OK.

Recovering Your Lost Password

To recover your password
  1. Navigate to the Web site.
  2. In the login form, click the Forgot Your Password? hyperlink.
    The User Information form appears.
  3. Fill in the required fields and click Submit.
    When the information is verified, you will receive a password through email.
