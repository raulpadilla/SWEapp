The registration process for this Web site is designed to address both anonymous and registered Web users. You can visit certain areas of the site anonymously, for example you can search the Frequently Asked Questions without registering. Registered users are granted expanded use of the Web site.

Self-Registering

To self-register
  1. Navigate to the home page.
  2. In the login form, click the New User hyperlink and follow the on-screen instructions.
    When the registration is complete the home page appears.

Accessing the Site Without Registering

To access the site without registering
  1. Point your browser to the Web site.
  2. Browse the Web site.
