A Develop Strategy establishes a position for a possible future engagement. When using a develop strategy, you work to position yourself for future wins..

Guidelines for implementing a develop strategy are:

  • Collect profile data (1-20) regularly. You should continue to collect profile data regarding the opportunity assessment, focusing on any changes since the last checkpoint.
  • Establish trust and credibility with senior executives. Senior executives know about opportunities before anybody else. You need to develop trust and credibility with senior executives so they will give you information that keeps you a step ahead of your competition.

Potential problems include:

  • Over-investment of resources (#18). Carefully assess the resources required to implement a develop strategy; it is easy to over-invest.
