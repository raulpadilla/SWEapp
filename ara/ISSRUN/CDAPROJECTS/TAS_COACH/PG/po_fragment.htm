Fragment Strategy Variations:

Fragment-Niche:
The objective of this variation is to get a foot in the door within a department, location or functional area. Elements may include:

  • Deparment/Location/Function
  • Foot in the door
  • Establish a beachhead

Fragment-Peacful Coexistence:
This variation focuses on your ability to expand an existing system with your solution. The solution will then peacefully coexist with the existing solution. Elements may include:

  • Compatibility with current environment
  • Extend capacity
  • Enhance customer’s investment (1+1=3)
